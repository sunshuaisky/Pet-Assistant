# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers using local AI coding tools who need a small desktop companion to monitor tool sessions, approve risky actions, answer lightweight status questions, and adjust pet preferences without leaving flow.

## Product Purpose

Phoenix Pet Island turns an animated desktop pet into a compact control surface for AI tool activity. Success means pending approvals and inputs are immediately visible, chat feels separate from monitoring, and settings are fast to scan without making the overlay feel heavy.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, companionable. The interface should feel like a trustworthy desktop utility with a warm mascot presence, not a marketing surface or novelty toy.

## Anti-references

Avoid orange-heavy alert styling, generic SaaS cards, decorative glassmorphism, dark-blue observability dashboards, oversized landing-page hierarchy, and playful UI that slows task completion.

## Design Principles

- The pet is the entry point, but the panel is a work surface.
- Monitoring, chat, and settings are separate modes with distinct mental models.
- Acrylic glass is functional atmosphere: it should create depth and focus, not obscure content.
- Attention color is reserved for work that needs the user.
- Familiar product controls beat novelty affordances.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support light, dark, and system themes. Provide reduced transparency and increased contrast options. Preserve keyboard access for pet activation and chat send behavior. Avoid relying on color alone for approval, input, success, or destructive states.
